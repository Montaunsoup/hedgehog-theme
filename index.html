Here are some reasons why hedgehogs are super cool:

  • Erinaceinae - Hedgehogs belong to a the family group Erinaceinae. They are not rodents but are more closely related to shrews, moles, and moonrats.
  • Nocturnal - They sleep during the day and are active during the night, so they can party all night.
  • Smile - Just look at that smile!
  • Cuddly - They can become cuddly when bonded with you.
  • Hypoallergenic - They hardly have any dander and are a great alternative to dogs and cats if you have allergies.
  • Longevity - They have a lifespan of about 7 years, which is a long time, comparatively, to small pets like rats.
  • Politically Active - They are so popular that in New Zealand a satirical political group tried to get a hedgehog elected to parliament.
  • Highly Influential - The famous blue rodent from Sonic the Hedgehog is indeed, a hedgehog.
  • Tubing - The act of domesticated hedgehogs getting their heads stuck in toiletpaper rolls is called tubing.
  • Prickle - A group of hedgehogs are called a prickle.
